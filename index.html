NBA career

Curry's NBA career has been going on since 2009. In the 2009 NBA draft, Stephen curry was selected by the Golden State Warriors in the 7th place in the first round and began his career

On February 2, 2011, the NBA officially announced the 2011 all star rookie challenge list, and Stephen curry was selected as the sophomore team

In the 2011-12 season, Stephen curry continued to suffer from injuries and played only 26 games

On April 20, 2013, Stephen curry was elected the best player in the West in April.

On December 8, 2013, in the game between warriors and Grizzlies, Stephen curry contributed 22 points, 15 assists and 6 rebounds, equating his career record of single game assists. In addition, curry surpassed Jason Richardson with two three-point hits and became the three-point champion of the warriors with 701 three-point hits.

On November 11, 2014, Stephen curry was elected player of the week for the first time in his career,On June 17, 2015, the warriors beat the Cavaliers 4-2 to win the 2014-15 NBA championship.

On May 11, 2016, Stephen curry was elected as the most valuable player in the regular season of 2015-16. Curry got all 131 first votes, becoming the first regular season most valuable player in NBA history to get all the first votes. On April 14, 2016, the warriors beat the Grizzlies 125-104 at home, becoming the first player in NBA history to break 400 three-point hits in a single season, helping the warriors break the Bulls' record (72-10) in 1995-96 season with 73-9 regular season record, and becoming the best team in NBA history.

On June 13, 2017, the warriors beat the Cavaliers 129-120 in the fifth game of the finals to win the 2016-17 NBA championship by 4-1. Stephen curry played 41 minutes, got 34 points, 6 rebounds and 10 assists. In his career, he accumulated 1965 points in the playoffs, tied with Robert holly, and tied for the 57th place in NBA playoff scoring history.

On June 9, 2018, the warriors won the Cavaliers 108-85 away with a total score of 4-0. This is the warriors' third championship in nearly four years, and the sixth championship in the team's history. They are tied with the bulls for the third place in history, behind the Celtics who won the 17th championship and the Lakers who won the 16th championship. Curry hit seven three points and slashed 37.

On October 30, 2018, the NBA officially announced that Stephen curry, the best player of Western Conference week of the second week, was elected.

On October 30, 2019, Stephen curry suffered a fracture of his left metacarpal bone during the game against the suns and then underwent surgery.
